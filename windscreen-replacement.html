Full Windscreen Replacement — Done Properly

A windscreen is a structural part of your vehicle. It helps support the roof and is the backing surface the passenger airbag deploys against. That's why we only do full replacements with correctly bonded, OEM-quality laminated glass — not quick fixes that leave you back at square one.

We source the correct glass for your exact vehicle and fit it to the manufacturer's specification. Most replacements take around two hours, and we'll tell you the safe drive-away time before we leave.

💡 Did you know? Most windscreens with a long crack, or damage in the driver's line of sight, can't be safely repaired — they need full replacement. We'll assess yours honestly and tell you what it actually needs.

What's Included

  • OEM-quality laminated glass matched to your vehicle's make, model and year
  • Correct urethane bonding with manufacturer-approved adhesives and full cure-time guidance
  • Sensor & camera transfer for vehicles with rain/light sensors and forward-facing cameras
  • New mouldings and clips where required so the seal is weather-tight
  • Clean-up and disposal of the old glass
  • Workmanship guarantee on every replacement

ADAS & Driver-Assist Cameras

Many newer vehicles have a forward-facing camera mounted to the windscreen for lane-keep assist and autonomous emergency braking. When the windscreen is replaced, these systems must be recalibrated so they read the road correctly. We'll advise whether yours needs recalibration and arrange it as part of the job.

Insurance or Private — Your Choice

Most of our work is insurance-based. We deal with all the major NZ insurers, manage the claim paperwork and invoice your insurer directly, so the only thing you pay is your excess (if any). Not insured? We'll give you a firm, fair price up front.
